3 of My Favorite All-Time Movies

The Lord of the Rings Trilogy

This is a beautiful cinematic depiction of J.R.R. Tolkien's written masterpiece.

Pride and Prejudice

This movie was my introduction to Jane Austen, which makes it one of my favorite movies ever.

Moonstruck

I love the depiction of a close-knit Italian-American family in New York. There's a certain "over-the-topness" in their interactions with each other.
